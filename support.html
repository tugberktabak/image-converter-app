How Image Converter Works

Image Converter allows you to convert, resize, and compress images directly on your device. All image processing is performed locally, and your files never leave your device.

Billing & Subscriptions

If you have questions about subscriptions, payments, renewals, or cancellations, please note that all billing is handled through the App Store and RevenueCat.

You can manage or cancel your subscription directly from your App Store account settings.

Technical Issues

If you experience crashes, unexpected behavior, or performance issues, please include the following information when contacting support:

  • Your device model and operating system version
  • The app version you are using
  • A short description of the issue and steps to reproduce it
